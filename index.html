Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about investing with DeAgro

What is the minimum investment amount?

+

Our standard minimum investment at this time is at $1,000 USD, allowing investors of various capacities to participate in agricultural opportunities.

When is AGX launch date?

+

We intend to launch AGX, DeAgro's main token, in Q4 2025. Then, we will send the due AGX directly to the investor's designated crypto wallet.

When can I expect returns on my investment?

+

Most investment packages offer quarterly distributions, with annual yields historically ranging between 8-12% depending on the specific agricultural sector and market conditions. There will be a lock up period for early investors due to the fact that is not feasible to have good returns in the early days of DeAgro's process, being the project a longer period investment in nature.

Can I invest from any country?

+

DeAgro accepts investors from most countries worldwide, subject to local regulations and KYC/AML laws. Our team will confirm eligibility during your consultation.
